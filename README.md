## Features
- Shake-to-roll (just like rolling a die!)
- Displays a delicious fast-food image on every shake
- Lightweight, smooth, connects you to food outlets closest to you

## How to Use
1. Open the app
2. Shake your phone (like you're shaking salt on fries!)
3. Enjoy your randomly selected fast-food fate

Great for:
- Deciding dinner with friends
- Breaking decision paralysis
- Just having fun

- Just give it a quick shmeat and tackle your empty tummy when you don't know what to eat

## Installation

### Prerequisites
- Flutter SDK (3.0 or higher)
- Android Studio / VS Code / Xcode (for iOS)
